Our Services

Kirk Home Inspections offers a comprehensive range of inspection services to ensure the safety and quality of your home.

  • Pre-Purchase Home Inspections

    Thorough inspections conducted before purchasing a home to identify any potential issues or defects.

  • Pre-Listing Home Inspections

    Inspections performed before listing a home for sale to identify any areas that may need attention before putting the property on the market.

  • New Construction Inspections

    Inspections carried out during the construction process to ensure that the home meets building codes and standards.

  • Radon Testing

    Testing for radon gas, a radioactive gas that can seep into homes and pose health risks to occupants.

  • Termite Inspections

    Inspections to detect the presence of termites and other wood-destroying pests that can cause structural damage to a home.

  • Additional Services

    In addition to our core services, we also offer a range of additional inspections and testing services tailored to your specific needs.
